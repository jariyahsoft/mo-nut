## Instructions

1. Prepare a synthetic-data usability protocol for profile, OCR, medication, measurement, caregiver permission, offline sync, install guidance, and SOS.
2. Test representative elderly/caregiver-assisted journeys; record completion, errors, comprehension, touch/zoom, permission, and trust concerns without unnecessary PHI.
3. Prioritize findings, revise prototypes/components/copy, and document accepted tradeoffs/open questions.

## Verify
- Protocol includes consent and safe data
- Each journey has observed evidence
- High-severity blockers are fixed/escalated
- Re-test confirms revisions
